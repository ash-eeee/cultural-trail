Huashan 1914 Creative Park

🏭

Huashan 1914 Creative Park was launched in Taiwan since 1914. Originally a winery, it was transformed into one of Taiwan's most celebrated cultural and creative spaces. The park houses galleries, performance venues, design studios, and local artisan shops, celebrating the intersection of history and modern creativity.
